Said Lot 47 is bounded Northerly by Belleclaire Avenue one hundred twen-
ty and six one hundredths (120.06) feet; Easterly by lot #45 on said plan

Said property is conveyed subject to the following restrictions which
are imposed upon said property for the benefit of my remaining premises and
of any heretofore conveyed by me. Said restrictions terminate January 1st,
1935 and are the following; that all buildings shall be placed and set
back at least twenty five (25) feet from the street line provided that steps
porticos and other projections appurtenant thereto are to be allowed in said
reserved space; that any dwelling house built upon said Lot 47 before the
termination of these restrictions shall cost not less than $5000. and on said
Lot 2 not less than $4000; that no more than one dwelling house shall be
erected on said lot; that no house shall be erected thereon for occupancy by
more than one family; that no building previously used shall be moved on to
said premises without the written permission of the grantor herein having
been first obtained; that all out buildings shall be clap boarded and painted
or shall be constructed of concrete; that no stables or henhouses shall be
built on said lots; that no garage shall be erected nearer than fifty feet
to the street line in front of said lots; that said lots shall not be resold
to a colored person, a Polander, or an Italian.
I, Hattie E. Robbins wife of said grantor release to said grantee all
rights of dower and homestead and other interests therein.
Witness our hands and seals this Fifth day of January 1917.
John McKean
