restrictions shall cost at least $4000.; that no more than one dwelling house
shall be erected on said lot; that no house shall be erected thereon for oc-

hen-houses shall be built on said lot; that no garage shall be erected thereon
nearer than fifty feet from the street line; that no old buildings shall be
moved on the said lot without the written permission of the grantor herein
having been first obtained; that said lot shall not be resold to an Italian,
a Polander or a colored person.
